# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Energy managers, plant managers, operators, and executives responsible for monitoring factory energy use, cost exposure, peak demand, production efficiency, and operating risk across Sonergy sites.

## Product Purpose

Sonergy Monitoring System helps industrial teams understand real-time and historical energy performance, identify abnormal usage, prioritize alerts, and compare areas of the factory so managers can make operational decisions with confidence.

## Brand Personality

Precise, calm, industrial.

The interface should feel professional and trustworthy for control-room and management review contexts. It should prioritize clear hierarchy, dense but readable data, and restrained status color.

## Anti-references

Avoid decorative SaaS dashboards, oversized marketing-style cards, vague abstract graphics, novelty controls, heavy gradients, and color that competes with operational status meaning.

## Design Principles

1. Lead with operational signal before decoration.
2. Use status color consistently for risk, production, warning, critical, and offline states.
3. Keep dashboards dense enough for managers, but preserve scan paths and spacing.
4. Make comparisons and exceptions easier to read than raw numbers.
5. Preserve familiar product UI patterns over novelty.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for dashboard text and status labels. Preserve visible focus states, deterministic rendering, and reduced-motion support for animated indicators.
